      Recommended diligence sequence

      From public evidence to a financeable case

      The next step is not to multiply a global headline estimate. It is to close the evidence gaps that materially affect a lender’s or buyer’s downside case.

      1. 01
        Reproduce the strongest annual datasets

        Archive query parameters, dated extracts and hashes for official sales, excise-volume and tax-revenue series.

      2. 02
        Reconcile market measures country by country

        Keep retail sales, manufacturer deliveries, customs value, taxable volume and modelled demand as separate columns.

      3. 03
        Map patent status and claim scope

        Verify national validation, renewals, claim charts, counterparties and product-specific relevance with patent counsel.

      4. 04
        Build lender and buyer scenarios

        Only after the evidence audit, link conservative, base and upside cases to legally supportable and realisable cash-flow assumptions.
