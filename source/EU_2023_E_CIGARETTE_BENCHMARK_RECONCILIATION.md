# European Union 2023 e-cigarette benchmark — source reconciliation

Status: institutionally supported secondary benchmark, not an accepted donor market
Review date: 2026-07-24

## Documents checked

1. European Commission, `SWD(2026) 111 final`, evaluation of the EU tobacco-control legislative framework:
   https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=SWD%3A2026%3A111%3AFIN
2. European Commission, `SWD(2025) 560 final`, impact assessment for the tobacco-excise framework:
   https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=SWD%3A2025%3A0560%3AFIN

The PDFs were downloaded from EUR-Lex and text-extracted locally. Relevant pages were rendered and checked visually against the original tables and notes.

## Cross-document result

`SWD(2026) 111`, Table 4 on physical PDF page 27 (printed page 26), reports:

| EU e-cigarette sales value | 2012 | 2023 |
|---|---:|---:|
| Sales value | EUR 909 million | EUR 4.99 billion |

The source note on physical PDF page 25 (printed page 24) states that the charts and tables on the following two pages come from `Euromonitor International Ltd, Tobacco, 2025 industry edition`.

`SWD(2025) 560`, Annex 13 on physical PDF pages 161–162 (printed pages 160–161), independently states that the EU aggregate grew from EUR 2.9 billion in 2020 to nearly EUR 5 billion in 2023. It defines the e-cigarette market as devices plus liquids to be vaporised, which may contain nicotine or be nicotine-free.

The annex also publishes these 2023 scope checks:

- single-use products: EUR 1.6 billion;
- rechargeable closed-system refills: EUR 416 million;
- liquids for open systems: EUR 1.74 billion;
- value shares: closed refills 41%, open-system liquids 35% and devices 25%;
- EU weighted average price: EUR 3.60/ml for closed refills and EUR 0.64/ml for open-system liquids.

The percentages are reproduced as printed and sum to 101%, so they should be treated as rounded shares.

## Evidence boundary

The Annex 13 chart identifies its source as an external study based on Euromonitor International and states that information is unavailable for Cyprus, Luxembourg and Malta. The public Commission documents do not provide a fully reusable country-level dataset, complete transaction definition, channel-by-country coverage, tax basis or a method sufficient to reproduce the EUR 4.99 billion total independently.

The value is therefore useful as institutional corroboration and a commercial-data cross-check. It is not:

- a primary official observation collected consistently from all 27 Member States;
- a complete public country table;
- an accepted comparable-country donor for the global model; or
- an independently reproduced global or EU estimate by this project.

## Finnish summary / Suomenkielinen yhteenveto

Komission SWD(2026) 111:n taulukko 4 raportoi EU:n vuoden 2023 sähkötupakkamarkkinan arvoksi 4,99 miljardia euroa. Lähdehuomautus yksilöi aineistoksi Euromonitor Internationalin Tobacco 2025 -toimialapainoksen. SWD(2025) 560:n liite 13 vahvistaa lähes 5 miljardin euron koonnin ja määrittelee rajaukseksi laitteet sekä nikotiinia sisältävät tai sisältämättömät nesteet.

Kyse on luotettavasta institutionaalisesta vertailupisteestä, mutta ei hyväksytystä luovuttajamarkkinasta: uudelleenkäytettävä maadata ja täydellinen menetelmä eivät ole julkisia, ja liitteen kuvaajasta puuttuvat Kyproksen, Luxemburgin ja Maltan tiedot.
