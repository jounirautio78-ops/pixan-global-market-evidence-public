# United States FTC reported-sales series, 2015–2021

Reviewed: 2026-07-24

## What the series measures

This public series adds the Federal Trade Commission table values for:

1. cartridge-system sales; and
2. disposable, non-refillable e-cigarette sales.

It intentionally excludes open-system sales so that the same two product
categories are used in every year. The result is an official-table arithmetic
series of reported manufacturer sales, not a complete consumer-retail
sell-through series and not an accepted donor market.

## Reproducible arithmetic

| Year | Cartridge systems (USD) | Disposables (USD) | Published series sum (USD) |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| 2015 | 259,984,551 | 44,185,495 | 304,170,046 |
| 2016 | 417,302,598 | 68,404,886 | 485,707,484 |
| 2017 | 707,415,500 | 72,420,899 | 779,836,399 |
| 2018 | 1,969,019,051 | 74,683,954 | 2,043,703,005 |
| 2019 | 2,633,333,620 | 69,274,687 | 2,702,608,307 |
| 2020 | 2,132,503,069 | 261,920,036 | 2,394,423,105 |
| 2021 | 2,496,219,204 | 267,065,134 | 2,763,284,338 |

Formula for every row:

`cartridge-system sales + disposable sales = published series sum`

## Comparability and donor boundary

- The 2015–2018 collection covered six then-leading domestic manufacturers.
- The 2019 collection covered the five original recipients still marketing
  e-cigarettes.
- The corrected 2020 table adds 2020 data from three of the four first-time
  recipients to the five prior recipients. The 2021 collection covers all nine
  recipients. The report warns that 2019 disposable sales are not comparable
  with 2020 or 2021 because companies focused on disposables were added.
- Open-system products are excluded from this derived series. FTC also noted
  that its earlier reporting manufacturers may represent only a small fraction
  of the open-system market.
- The figures combine reported direct and indirect manufacturer sales. They do
  not establish complete national retail-channel coverage, a consumer
  sell-through definition, VAT or sales-tax treatment, or an independent
  reconciliation.

The series therefore passes the public-source and reproducibility checks, but
it remains outside the accepted donor count.
